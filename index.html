ARANMOR GUEST HOUSE

Welcome to Aranmor Guest House, nestled on a sprawling 20-acre estate overlooking the breathtaking coastline of Aran Island.

Welcome to Aranmor Guest House, where nature's grandeur takes center stage. Perched on the cliffs of Kilronan, our retreat offers unparalleled panoramic views of the boundless Atlantic Ocean. Just a leisurely stroll from the iconic Joe Wattys and a brief 20-minute walk from the ferry, our cozy haven immerses you in the rugged beauty of the Irish coastline. Step into our modern, nature-inspired spaces, where every comfort is seamlessly woven into the fabric of the surroundings, promising an unforgettable stay amidst the untamed beauty of the ocean.

ROOMS & SUITES

Our Guest House features finely appointed accommodations, including four romantic guest rooms and two suites. For more privacy, we also offer two stand-alone units: our historic Darrell Hill Cottage and our newly-renovated Red Barn. Each room has its own distinct style, yet they all feature comfortable sitting areas, private baths, exquisite design detail, original artwork and charming amenities including overhead and tower fans and the cooler temperatures that come with our altitude. Enjoy the breeze without the need for air conditioning.

COUNTRY ESTATE

At 1,400 feet, the Estate offers sweeping ocean views from every corner. Explore the open-air floor plan of the main house, where natural Atlantic breezes. Outside, there is a mature lawned garden where you and your guests can relax and enjoy the peaceful sea views. BBQ is available under request.Wifi is available at the property. Towels are provided within the house Local area & Tourist Attractions Bealadangan is a small Gaeltacht village with a grocery store, post office and village pub. With free private parking, We are located on the west side of the island, approximately 6.5km from the village of Kilronan.

ISLAND ACTIVITIES

The Wormhole is a rectangular shaped blow hole that was naturally carved by mother nature. It’s dimensions are approximately 25m long, 10m wide and between 10-20m deep. The Wormhole or “Poll na bPeist” in Gaelic is a spectacular natural feature, and has become one of the main attractions on Inishmore since the Red Bull Cliff Diving World Series was held here in 2014 & 2017. Once you’ve booked your stay with us, contact aranmorinn@gmail.com for personal planning assistance.

FRESH BREAKFAST

Mornings at our Big Island retreat are a wondrous experience. Our Full Irish Breakfast is a beloved favourite among locals and visitors alike. Savour the hearty flavours of sizzling bacon, succulent sausages, perfectly cooked eggs, grilled tomatoes, and crispy hash browns. Accompanied by black and white puddings, baked beans, and buttery toast, it’s a feast fit for champions. Perfect to set you up for a day exploring all the sights of Inis Mór.For those seeking a lighter option, our Continental Breakfast is an excellent choice. Enjoy a delightful selection of freshly baked pastries, crusty bread, a variety of cheeses, cold cuts, yoghurt, seasonal fruits, and a range of cereals. Complement your meal with a steaming cup of freshly brewed coffee or a refreshing glass of fruit juice.
